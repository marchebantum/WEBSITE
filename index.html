Kyber

Kyber is our proprietary product intelligence platform.

Kyber automates product development workflows, compliance requirements, feature prioritisation and pricing optimisation. AI agents analyse customer feedback, predict product-market fit, and generate rapid prototypes, compressing your idea-to-launch cycles.

Secure, modular architecture is customised around your existing design and delivery tools. With Kyber, every offering you create is data-informed, regulation-compliant, and competitively priced.

Lotus

Lotus is our AI-powered organic brand engine.

Lotus automates creative ideation, photorealistic content rendering, and social media scheduling. AI agents learn your style and dynamically render visuals. We automate lead generation through chatbots deployed across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Messenger, engaging your audience, qualifying leads, and booking meetings directly through integrated calendar links. Intrepid

Intrepid is our proprietary sales and commission platform.

Intrepid automates invoice creation, customer data entry, payment processing, and commission tracking into a unified dashboard. Eliminate human error via auto-filled invoices and customer details, payment processing, and auto-verified commission claims.

Audit trails ensure governance and compliance, while real-time analytics and revenue forecasts. Secure integrations with your CRM and finance tools ensure every sale is captured, tracked, and compensated accurately.

Kessel

Kessel is a fully personalised operating system.

Kessel gives every user a custom dashboard tailored to their workflow. Live chat and natural language voice agents control chat-based task, reminder and calendar creation, helping you ideate and problem-solve in real-time. Kessel runs effortlessly, connecting individual dashboards across your team, syncing tasks, deadlines, and dependencies automatically.

AI agents handle data entry and document generation, so individuals are freed to focus on what they do best.

ZENIAS

Zen Inspired Agentic Systems

Zenias was founded by Marche Bantum, an artist, lawyer and entrepreneur from Australia. After scaling a marketing company built on high-quality commercial production and software and culminating in a major vendor acquisition, the journey into software development became inevitable. As an extremely early adopter of AI, the gap became clear: businesses need automated systems, but they also need to look and feel amazing.

Observing the incredible democratisation of technology through AI, Marche assembled a team of passionate developers and designers to help people and organisations build their next chapter of digital success. As a passionate educator, his belief is simple: everyone has the right to learn these tools and build their Next.

Digital Renaissance is our central philosophy. It is where the craft of classical engineering is reborn in modern computation. We are living through the digital renaissance at this very moment. Zenias is your thought partner and collaborator throughout this time of incredible opportunity and change.

Where craft meets code.

The Problem

Mass adoption has stripped software of personality. Systems are rigid, impersonal, and built for the average user – not your personalised workflow. Software should fit you, not force you to adapt. Zenias rejects the template. We build systems that move with you, shaped around how you think and work.

Our Mission

Our mission is to architect AI-powered systems that respect human judgment, creative intuition, and operational sovereignty. We believe automation should enhance, but never replace, the craft. Digital Renaissance is our philosophy: timeless principles of design, precision, and beauty, applied to intelligent systems that evolve with you.

Our Team

Our team comprises of software developers, data security experts, designers and creatives. We coverd every core pillar of AI and agentic systems, from architecture and compliance to interface design and deployment. We focus on building experiences that feel effortless, secure, and entirely yours.

Who We Serve

We serve anyone willing to learn and embrace the future. Commercial teams. Sole traders. Creative studios. Founders. Educators. If you believe software should work for you, then Zenias is for you.
